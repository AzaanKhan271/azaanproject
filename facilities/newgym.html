Grind Karachi Gym: A new Gym with Weights Awaits you!

Grind karachi is about to open a new gym equipped with all the necessary items for weight training.


From free weights to machinery the new gym will have all the types of equipment. In addition to that, the existing gym will have a fighting cage too!

The Grind team will now enjoy training with weights under the same roof. In addition to the MMA equipment the new gym will allow members to explore the weightlifting in a fighters way.

The coaches will guide on using the weights in order to gain muscle as well as to prepare for fights more efficiently.

This gym will be open to both genders and trainers will guide the beginners and newbies on how to proceed.

The need for weight training for athletes is a must. In order to get full strength, an MMA fighter can not neglect the need to lift.

Grind Karachi Gym is opening soon. The enthusiasts are advised to keep in touch with the administration as we will be allowing only limited slots on first come first serve basis.

Act now to get your seat reserved!
