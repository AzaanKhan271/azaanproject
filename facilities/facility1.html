Grind: Facilities and Training Programs

Grind invites you to join one of its kind training program in Karachi. Under one roof a person can train Judo, Brazilian Jujitsu, Grappling, and even Gymnastics!

One of its kind initiative allows students to reach their full potential. Regular seminars and workshops from leading players in the field make it unique.

State of the art facility ensures the availability of all the necessary stuff required to perform workouts. Strict quality control has been observed in choice of training material and workout equipment.

Olympians having expertise in all fighting genres are available for learners. Contrary to traditional norms, this club provides most of the martial arts training simultaneously.

Being the biggest MMA facility in the country, Grind presents a unique opportunity for combat sports enthusiasts. In the absence of proper MMA training institutes in Pakistan its a commendable effort.

Another good point about this place is that it provides a training facility for females under the supervision of Lady instructors Children can also train under certified trainers. Regular classes of Zumba and Fitness for women are held at the campus.

The best thing for learners arena is that it is ideal for working men and women. Grind has many time slots available for the convenience of the students. Apart from regular classes private trainings facility is also availible. One can train under the acclaimed masters with ease of schedule.
